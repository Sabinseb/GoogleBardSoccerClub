FCC Soccer Club

The FCC Soccer Club is a passionate group of players dedicated to the beautiful game. We provide a fun and competitive environment for players of all skill levels to develop their talents and love for the sport. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or just starting out, we welcome you to join our team.

Here are some details about our club:

  • Age groups: U8 - U18
  • Practice schedule: Tuesdays and Thursdays, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
  • Location: Central Park Soccer Field
  • Experienced coaching staff
